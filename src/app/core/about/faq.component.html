Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is registering on the platform free?

Yes. Mobispace does not charge its users any fees or ongoing costs for usage of the platform.

Can I post ads on the platform if I'm not a registered user?

No. Posting ads on the marketplace requires users to be registered and logged in. Additional functionality will unlock once the user is logged in.

Is there a limit to how many ads I can post at any one time?

No. There is no limit on how many ads can be posted by a single user at any given time, but ads that get reported as fake or spam will be removed.

Am I allowed to contact sellers if I'm not a registered user?

Absolutely. You can contact sellers without being registered.

How can I change my account information and settings?

Once logged in, you can click on the gear icon to access your personal information. You are able to change your personal details from there.

I have come across fake or misleading ads on the marketplace. What do I do?

Please contact us via our official channels and we will investigate accordingly.

Does the platform offer any monetary services or transactions of any kind?

No. Mobispace does not handle any payments, transactions or other operations.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can delete your account via the gear icon menu once logged in. Do take note that once deleted, this cannot be reversed and all posted ads on the marketplace will be deleted as well.
